Speech to text

نَسْخ · Naskh

Turn a long Arabic recording into text, entirely in your browser. The audio never touches any server except Groq's transcription endpoint.

Transcript
Uses Whisper large-v3 for best Arabic accuracy. Files under ~24 MB are sent whole;
longer ones are cut into parts without re-encoding, so splitting takes seconds.
Finished parts are remembered — if anything dies, Transcribe again resumes where it left off.
